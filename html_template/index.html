Air & Water Barrier System

Protection and Performance Inside & Out

The ForceField® air and water barrier system consists of engineered wood sheathing panels which are laminated with a proprietary air and water barrier. The panels install just like standard OSB or plywood sheathing. Once installed and taped with ForceField seam tape, the system creates an integrated air and water barrier for your structure, eliminating the need for separate house wrap. When you do away with this step it helps save you time and labor costs, which ultimately lowers your total installed costs. The ForceField system is ideal for residential or light commercial construction.

ForceField® Panels

Not Just Another Pretty Face.

ForceField panels are part of a vapor permeable system which protects your structure immediately upon installation. These panels install just like standard OSB or plywood and have an edge seal to help reduce water intrusion. Panels are available in 8-foot, 9-foot and 10-foot lengths.

ForceField® Seam Tape

Seems Too Good To Be True.

ForceField seam tape is a highly advanced acrylic tape which is thin and easy to work with, yet extremely strong and durable. Because the ForceField overlay is adhered to the smooth side of our OSB panel, our seam tape doesn’t have to be thick and difficult to cut. Now it’s easy to seal seams and joints with your own hands to provide excellent air and moisture protection. Performs in cold weather down to 0°F. ForceField seam tape is available in 180-foot rolls.

ForceFieldTM Corner Seal

We Have Air & Moisture Covered

ForceField corner seal gives you a fast, effective, innovative and protective solution for finishing inside and outside exterior corners. Once installed, ForceField corner seal helps provide additional protection against air and moisture intrusion in traditionally some of the most difficult areas of a home to seal. Corner seal is available in 200-foot rolls.

ForceFieldTM Flex Tape

Peel. Stretch. Stick. Smile. It’s that easy.

If you are used to standard flashing tape that doesn’t stretch or flex, ForceField flexible flashing tape will be a revelation. It wraps easily around all window openings and curved details without any extra cutting. Peel, stretch, stick and smile. It’s that easy. Flex tape is available in 6-inch and 9-inch widths.
