ForceField® Seam Tape

Seems Too Good To Be True.

ForceField seam tape has a highly modified acrylic adhesive designed to work at temperatures as low as 0°F. This means you don’t have to worry about work being delayed in extremely cold temperatures. Also, ForceField tape not only creates a strong bond, but is light in weight and is serrated so it’s easy to tear. No utility knives or special applicators are necessary. UV and weather resistant, it comes in convenient 180 foot rolls, so you don’t have to worry about running out of tape so often.

Advanced Technology In A Thin, Premium Seam Tape

ForceField seam tape is a highly advanced acrylic tape that is thin and easy to work with yet extremely strong a durable. ForceField seam tape seals seams and joints to provide excellent air and moisture protection and is ideal for cold weather and improving energy efficiency.

  • Thin profile seam tape is easier to work with than traditional flashing-type tapes
  • Pressure sensitive tape, tear and apply with your hands
  • Serrated edges, no special tools needed to cut or install
  • UV and weather resistant
  • No release liner, reducing waste on the job site and helping save time during installation
  • Works in temperatures down to 0°F
  • Thin profile helps shed water easier than thicker tapes
  • Tape technology that has been around more than 30 years
  • Strong, durable air and moisture protection

Smooth Side Application for Improved Adhesion

Most don’t realize there are two sides to an OSB panel. The “screen” side and the “smooth” side. The screen side is rough. Look closely and you’ll see for yourself. The smooth side is…well…smooth. Because the ForceField overlay is adhered to the smooth side of the panel, the tape for the seams doesn’t need to be thick. We designed ForceField on the smooth side for a reason: So our seam tape can be thin and easy to work with. It then creates the intense bond between the board and the tape without the worry of gaps that could happen if taping was done on the screen side. The secret is out. Smooth is in.

Take It To The House

The ForceField system eliminates the need for house wrap. When you do away with this step it helps save you time and labor costs, which ultimately lowers your total installed cost. ForceField requires only one installer to tape the seams between the panels. House wrap takes at least two installers. Plus, house wrap requires special attention for window and corner details, whereas ForceField makes it easy to flash windows, doors and other penetrations. You’ll never have to deal with re-working blown off house wrap again.
